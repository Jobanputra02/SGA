Our Video Shootings

We are glad to inform you that we have started to flex in your YouTube platform. We had put numerous efforts for this achievement, the entire arrangements and efforts was done by students only. Our first video was on Sacred Games theme. In frame you can see Karan Tanna, Yash Kotak, Vidhi Sheth, Drashti Jivrajani, Akshesh Pithva, Chaitanya Jobanputra, Jayraj Dhakan and Vishva Bhatt in guitars. And ofcourse our back-end allies were Yash Kotak and Karan Tanna. And our second video was on Vande Mataram theme to honour our great leaders on the historic republic day. In frame you can catch Vishw Sheth, Karan Tanna and Yash Kotak on strings and Bhaumik Shah on drums. And ofcourse our back-end allies were Yash Kotak and Karan Tanna. We hope to present you more and more videos by the YouTube platform. Keep supporting and rock on.

Into The State & National Levels

Sargam Guitar Academy participated in many contests including districts, states and national levels. Our student Yash Kotak secured first position in Indian Youth Festival held on December 2018 (District Level), Intercity music festival 2019 (Zonal) and also performed on Youth Festival of 2018 (State Level). Our another student Jyoti G. also secured rank at Indian Youth Festival 2019 at national level.
