The Sargam Guitar Academy envisions a society where everyone have access to the unique social, emotional, intellectual benefits achieved through musical excellence. We believe music is transformative and empowers the lives of youth, families and communities. SGA aspires to assure the the highest standard of music education and to value and celebrate the excellence in teaching and innovation achieved by the academy's tutors.

To educate, develop and inspire students to achieve their full musical potential and provide them the skills for lifelong enjoyment of music. Sargam Guitar Academy is committed to the highest standards of musicianship by working with highly experienced tutors, combining innovative and individualized programs to meet the needs of our students and families thereby contributing to their musical success.

Our approach for this academy is to promote the highest levels of human aspiration and artistic integrity through the composition, documentation, and performance of music and to develop an analytical, creative, and intuitive understanding of music as a cultural language. We are tightly bounded to afford students opportunities for cultural and aesthetic experiences through active participation in music and enhance it.
